Last updated: 04/24/2026

Images Optimizer and Videos Optimizer (“the Apps”) are originally developed by chaotg and currently maintained by DevToolsOwner (formerly TheSoccer).
This privacy policy explains how the Apps handle user data.

1. Information We Collect
The Apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
The Apps operate entirely on the user’s device and do not require account creation, login, or identity information.

2. Local Device Data
Any data generated by the Apps (such as processed images, settings, or preferences, temporary files) are stored locally on the user’s device.
This data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

3. Internet Access
The Apps work entirely offline and do not require an internet connection for any of its features.
The Apps do not send or receive data over the internet under any circumstances.

4. Third‑Party Services
The Apps do not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external SDKs that collect personal data.

5. Children’s Privacy
Because the Apps do not collect personal information, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages.
